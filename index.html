About Us

Pure Protection, Naturally

Elairich Bio Solutions develops and manufactures liquid biological fungicides, insecticides and bio-fertilizers built on living microbial cultures — an organic alternative to chemical inputs for modern farming.

Every product is formulated with standardized CFU counts, so growers get consistent, dependable results for disease control, pest management and soil health, batch after batch.

  • Organic solutions for plant growth and disease control
  • Eco-friendly biological control of pests, fungi and nematodes
  • Bio-fertilizers that fix nitrogen and unlock phosphorus & potash
  • Promotes sustainable, long-term soil fertility
